Welcome to Séance

The story...

Having graduated from college, Cass was in desperate need of a job. He lived with his parents, who constantly reminded him that his life was going nowhere -- a fact that he was fully aware of.

Eventually he got desperate and responded to a seedy job offer, unwittingly selling his soul and becoming a maid for witches, ghouls, ghosts, and everthing you’ve ever feared.

Whether he’s good or not at his job is up to you.

How to Stay Alive

In Seance, you play as a character who is trapped in a haunted mansion with various spirits and monsters. You need to survive until dawn in order to win the game, and in order to survive you need to keep the guests of this mansion satisfied with offerings, which you must aim at the guests, and accommodations such as compatibility with neighboring monsters. If you neglect just one guest, they will become agitated and begin agitating others around the mansion.

If the player’s health reaches 0 before the end of the night, you lose.
